From 12 November to 14 November 2025 JITEL (Jornadas de Ingeniería Telemática) took place, where Ramón Sánchez Iborra from the Universidad de Murcia presented three papers. The event was organized by SCITEL with the mission to unite groups specializing in teaching and research related to telematics networks and services. It brought together over 100 researchers and university groups creating a Spanish forum for discussing the cutting edge of telecom innovation.

The thematic areas and talks by Ramón Sánchez Iborra were:
- VRS (Network and Service Virtualization): *eBPF-Hub: gestor de funciones eBPF* addressing how eBPF (extended Berkeley Packet Filter) technology is used to manage networks dynamically and with high performance.
- DT(Teaching in Telematics): *Aula invertida en docencia remota a nivel de máster: experiencia y 
resultados obtenidos* and *Aprendizaje learning by doing y evaluación sin estrés para la enseñanza de 
protocolos de enrutamiento*.
