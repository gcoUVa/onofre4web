On 31 October in Cartagena, Pablo Pavón and Victoria Bueno participated in the Talent+Forum 2025. The event was organized by the Universidad Politécnica de Cartagena (UPCT) with the goal of fostering innovation, talent, and entrepreneurship. The forum served as a meeting point for entrepreneurs, companies, institutions and entities that focused on Smart Cities as both a challenge and an opportunity. 
During the event, advances in the ONOFRE-4 project, related to applications for smart cities, particularly air quality monitoring, were presented.
